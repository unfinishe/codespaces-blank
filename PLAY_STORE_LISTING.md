## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Android Pic Sort – Organize Your Photos by Date

Effortlessly organize your photo library on Android.

Android Pic Sort is a fast, simple, and privacy-first photo organizer for Android 15+. Sort photos into a date-based folder structure (YYYY/MM MonthName) directly on your device.

**Key Features:**
✓ Copy or Move – Choose how to organize your files
✓ Offline – 100% private, no internet required
✓ Smart Date Detection – EXIF metadata with automatic fallback to file creation date
✓ Dry Run Mode – Preview changes before committing
✓ Conflict Resolution – Choose to rename or overwrite duplicates
✓ Video Support – Optional sorting of videos and non-image media
✓ Detailed Report – See exactly what was organized

**Privacy First:**
• No data collection or analytics
• No tracking or advertising
• No cloud sync or account required
• All processing happens on your device

**Language Support:**
🇬🇧 English
🇩🇪 Deutsch (automatically selected based on your device locale)

**Supported Formats:**
Images: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, HEIC, HEIF, DNG
Videos: MP4, MKV, MOV, WebM, and more

**How It Works:**
1. Select your source folder (where your photos are)
2. Select your target folder (where you want them organized)
3. Choose your preferences (copy/move, date source, dry run, etc.)
4. Run – the app organizes your files
5. Review the report to confirm the operation

**Requirements:**
• Android 15 (API 35) or higher
• ~50 MB storage space
• Storage Access Framework (SAF) permissions

**Performance:**
Optimal for photo libraries with fewer than 10,000 files. Larger libraries may take several minutes depending on your device's processing power and EXIF availability.

**Open Source:**
Android Pic Sort is open-source and available on GitHub:
https://github.com/unfinishe/andro-pic-sort

**Support & Feedback:**
Have a question or found a bug? Open an issue on GitHub:
https://github.com/unfinishe/andro-pic-sort/issues

**Privacy Policy:**
For details on data handling and privacy, see:
PRIVACY_POLICY.md in the project repository

**License:**
MIT License – Free and open-source
```

## Privacy & Permissions Declaration

**Does the app collect sensitive user data?**  
No. The app does not collect, transmit, or store personal data.

**What permissions are requested?**  
- `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` / `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` via Storage Access Framework (SAF)  
- **Purpose**: To access and organize user-selected photo folders

**Does the app use third-party services?**  
No. The app operates 100% offline with no external integrations.
